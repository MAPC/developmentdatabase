Development Database

Development Database is a collaborative data inventory of past, present and future real estate development in the Boston metro region.

Why DEVELOPMENT DATABASE

Metro Boston is experiencing a wave of growth signaling a new era in the region’s history. Understanding this growth is essential to planning for an equitable, prosperous, and sustainable future.

This data inventory is designed to help analysts, planners, real estate developers and community advocates understand development in our region by compiling reliable, project-level data on past, future and current development projects. Development Database currently encompasses 3,500 projects with attributes including housing units and commercial square footage, status, year of completion, parking availability, proximity to transit, WalkScore™ and other aggregate data for the region.
